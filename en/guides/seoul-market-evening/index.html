Market And DDP

City: Seoul

Open in app

Overview

The evening follows a short line: Cheonggyecheon lowers the center noise, Gwangjang supplies the market meal, Heunginjimun gives a historic marker, and DDP finishes the walk by a modern plaza and metro.

Crowds can tire you fast here. Pick one market seat, keep your bag close, and do not add another distant district after DDP; evening transport and fatigue eat into the buffer quickly.
