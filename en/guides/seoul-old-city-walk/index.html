Deoksugung And Seochon

City: Seoul

Open in app

Overview

This route steps away from the most obvious palace axis: Deoksugung by City Hall, the Seoul Museum of History, quiet Gyeonghuigung, Tongin Market in Seochon, and Insadong for the finish. It has fewer must-see signals and more small decisions along the way.

Do not turn the half-day into a museum marathon. If Deoksugung or the city museum takes longer, keep Gyeonghuigung brief and reach the market before fatigue makes the return east unpleasant.
