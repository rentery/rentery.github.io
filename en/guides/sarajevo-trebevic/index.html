Trebevic

City: Sarajevo

Open in app

Overview

Trebevic is better as its own half-day. The cable car lifts you quickly from Bistrik to the wooded slope, the viewpoint shows Sarajevo in its valley, and the Olympic bobsleigh track adds an important but not casual historical layer.

Do not combine this route with the fortresses above the old city or with distant museums. Weather, maintenance, and path conditions matter more than the number of stops; if visibility is poor, rebuild the day around the center.
