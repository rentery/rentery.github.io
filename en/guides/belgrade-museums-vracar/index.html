Museums And Vracar

City: Belgrade

Open in app

Overview

This day is for more content and less wandering. Start in the center, then transfer to the Nikola Tesla Museum and Saint Sava Temple, with the Museum of Yugoslavia in Dedinje as the finish.

Do not try to walk the whole route. Between Vracar and Dedinje, transport is the better choice. Check every museum in the morning, because Monday closures and special changes can easily break the order.
