Yongsan Museums

City: Seoul

Open in app

Overview

This day helps when streets are not helping: rain, heat, fine dust, or simple fatigue. Start at the National Museum, step out for brief air in Yongsan Family Park, then choose the War Memorial, Leeum, and a small Itaewon finish.

The route does not need to be fully walkable. Yongsan distances look tolerable on the map, but after galleries it is better to use metro, bus, or taxi than turn the day into a tiring transfer.
