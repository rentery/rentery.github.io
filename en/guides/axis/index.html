Beijing Axis

City: Beijing

Open in app

Overview

Move through the center from south to north: the open stone of Tiananmen, the red mass of Meridian Gate, the Palace Museum platforms, the garden by the northern exit, and Jingshan Hill. The route works because it stays on a line: the city first shows power through space, then through gates, then through roofs that finally align from above.

Logistics matter here, but do not let them become the whole story. Check square access and the Palace Museum ticket before leaving, then use your attention for scale, roof details, and the moment when avenue noise disappears behind palace walls.
