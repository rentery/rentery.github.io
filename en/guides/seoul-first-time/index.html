First Seoul

City: Seoul

Open in app

Overview

This day keeps the old center together without distant transfers: Gyeongbokgung, the museum by the palace edge, residential Bukchon, Jogyesa, and Cheonggyecheon. Move from large gates to narrow streets, then to the water where it is easy to finish by metro.

The main risk is arriving late and meeting queues, heat, or crowded lanes all at once. If your start shifts toward midday, shorten the palace or Bukchon instead of dragging the whole route through the crowd.
