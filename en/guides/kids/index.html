Pandas And Aquarium

City: Beijing

Open in app

Overview

This route does not try to cover the whole zoo. Enter, mark toilets and food, go to pandas, then take a short loop by energy and move to Beijing Aquarium if heat, rain, or crowds start pressing.

Tell children the honest version in advance: pandas do not have to be active. If nothing is visible at the glass, do not stand still for twenty minutes; shade, water, toilets, and a clear exit save the day.
